## A5


### Function

- A5 shows distinct functional connectivity differences
- A5 is more activated in language-related contrasts (LANGUAGE MATH, LANGUAGE STORY-MATH, STORY)
- A5 is less activated in working memory + relational contrasts
- A5 is also less activated in faces, TOM-random, cue contrasts (relative to STSdp/STSda)
- relative to STGa: A5 also has higher myelin + different FC
