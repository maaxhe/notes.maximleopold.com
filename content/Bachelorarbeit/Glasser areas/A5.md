## A5

### Function

- zeigt functional connectivity unterschiede zu Nachbarn
- is more activated in language-related contrasts (LANGUAGE MATH, LANGUAGE STORY-MATH, STORY)
- is less activated in working memory + relational contrasts
- is also less activated in faces, cue contrasts (relativ zu STSdp/STSda)
- relative to STGa: A5 also has higher myelin + different FC
  Quelle: [[Glasser et al. (2016) - Nature]]
