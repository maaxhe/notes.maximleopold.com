## Brain Hemispheres
### Left Hemisphere: Positive Emotions
- Newborns: with palatable sugar solution: higher EEG activation on the left
- 10-month-old children: when presented with happy faces: higher left hemisphere activity
- Manic patients: stronger left frontal EEG activity

### Right Hemisphere: Negative Emotions
- Newborns: with aversive lemon solution higher EEG activation on the right
- Depressed patients: stronger right frontal EEG activity
- Patients with **right hemisphere lesions**: cannot recognize fear, but can recognize joy
