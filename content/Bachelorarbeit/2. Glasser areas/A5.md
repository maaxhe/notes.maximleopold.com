## A5


### Function

- zeigt functional connectivity unterschiede zu Nachbarn ([[Glasser et al. (2016) - Nature]])
- is more activated in language-related contrasts (LANGUAGE MATH, LANGUAGE STORY-MATH, STORY) ([[Glasser et al. (2016) - Nature]])
- is less activated in working memory + relational contrasts ([[Glasser et al. (2016) - Nature]])
- is also less activated in faces, cue contrasts (relativ zu STSdp/STSda) ([[Glasser et al. (2016) - Nature]])
- relative to STGa: A5 also has higher myelin + different FC ([[Glasser et al. (2016) - Nature]])
