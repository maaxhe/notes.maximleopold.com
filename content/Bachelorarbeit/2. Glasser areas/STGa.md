### Location
- on [[PPo]] and STG anterior to [[Herschl's Gyrus]] (also [[TA2]]) ([[Ahveninen et al. (2006) - PNAS#^4f3ab3]])

### Functions
- part of the [[Ventral Auditory Stream]] ([[Ahveninen et al. (2006) - PNAS#^4f3ab3]])
- more activated in LANGUAGE STORY-MATH and TOM-RANDOM compared to [[TA2]] ([[Glasser et al. (2016) - Nature]])
- realtive to TGd is more activated in STORY and CUE-AVG ([[Glasser et al. (2016) - Nature]])

#### left STGa

### Connectivity 
- to [[IFG]] via strong [[Ventral Auditory Stream]] consisting of the inferior longitudinal fasciculus (ILF) and the inferior fronto-occipital fasciculus (IFOF) in its posterior portion, and the extreme capsule (EmC) in the anterior portion ([[Fr√ºhholz (2015) - NeuroImage#^0b4b98]])
- to [[IFG]] also via [[Dorsal Auditory Stream]], but higher pathway probability via ventral compared to dorsal ([[Fr√ºhholz (2015) - NeuroImage#^b33417]])
