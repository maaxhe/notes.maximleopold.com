## Anatomical location
a region mainly on the [[STG]] and within the [[STS]] that is activated in the LANGUAGE STORY, MATH, and STORY-MATH contrasts. ([[Glasser et al. (2016) - Nature#^476d27]])

## Function(s)
- becomes likely progressively less purely auditory and more multi-modal as one progresses inferiorly, anteriorly, and posteriorly ([[Glasser et al. (2016) - Nature#^68a319]])

## Connectivity
- strong connection to [[IFG]], including areas [[BA44, 44]], [[BA45, 45]], [[BA47l, 47l]] ([[Glasser et al. (2016) - Nature#^826543]])
